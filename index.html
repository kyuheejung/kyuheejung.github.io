Research

Social Psychology & Human Behavior

I conduct research on social comparison, inequality, and moral-related emotions such as schadenfreude and envy.

Forensic Psychology & Criminal Behavior

I conduct research on analyzing criminal behavior patterns and developing forensic psychological assessment tools. I particularly analyze psychosocial factors related to child abuse fatalities based on forensic autopsy data.

Facial Expression & Emotion Recognition

I conduct research on emotion recognition through facial expressions and their psychological meanings. I particularly develop facial expression analysis technologies related to deception detection.
